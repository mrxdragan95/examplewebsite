About

Our mission with the website Fitness@Home is to provide good ideas of various exercises for people who have to stay at home during the COVID-19 pandemic. Fitness@Home is any time exercise and fitness, just a button away. We are also offering diet charts depending of your plan whether to gain muscles or lose weight. Our motto is: “in healthy body is a healthy spirit” so we can help you to focus on more important things in your life.
